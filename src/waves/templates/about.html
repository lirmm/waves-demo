The Wonderful People Behind the Site

The Montpellier Laboratory of Informatics, Robotics and Microelectronics LIRMM) is attached to Montpellier University and the French National Center for Scientic Research (CNRS). Its activity is developed within three scientific research departments (Informatics, Microelectronics, Robotics), 19 team-projects and several common services.

The National Center for Scientific Research, or CNRS, is a public organization under the responsibility of the French Ministry of Education and Research.

The French Institute of Bioinformatics (referred to as IFB hereafter) is a national service infrastructure in bioinformatics that was created following the call for proposals, “National Infrastructures in Biology and Health”, of the “Investments for the Future” initiative.
